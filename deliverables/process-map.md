# Process Map — Henderson & Associates Client Intake

| # | Step | Automate / Keep Human / Hybrid | Justification |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Receptionist (Tamika) collects caller's basic info (name, phone, accident type, date) and checks statute of limitations | Hybrid | AI can capture intake details via a web form or phone bot and flag SOL status automatically, but a human should confirm edge cases like workers' comp and med mal deadlines. |
| 2 | Senior paralegal (Maria) conducts screening call to assess case viability | Hybrid | AI can run the standard 8–10 screening questions and flag weak cases, but a human should make the final keep/reject call given legal judgment and the firm's relationship-first culture. |
